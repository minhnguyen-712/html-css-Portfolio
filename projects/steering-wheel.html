Steering Wheel Redesign

Ergonomic Design CAD Modeling Fabrication

Full assembly front

Full assembly back

Problem: Driver feedback indicated that the previous steering wheel grips were uncomfortable and had a short lifespan. A new, more ergonomic and durable grip was needed.
My Contribution: I contributed to the ergonomic refinement of the steering wheel. I used driver feedback and research to inform a new 3D-printed grip design. This new design focused on reducing the circumference to improve driver comfort and responsive handling
