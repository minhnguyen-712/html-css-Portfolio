Pedal Box Assembly

Result: Reduced pedal effort and improved control linearity.

Design and optimization of the pedal box assembly with emphasis on precise kinematics and ergonomics for driver control. The work included linkage design, return spring selection, and mounting architecture.

Key Outcomes

  • Optimized linkage geometry to reduce pedal travel
  • Improved linearity and reduced hysteresis in pedal feel
  • Designed easy maintenance access for serviceability
