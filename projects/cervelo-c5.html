Project Overview

A comprehensive bicycle assembly and optimization project focusing on creating the perfect balance between performance and comfort. This project involved extensive research, precise measurements, and careful component selection to achieve optimal results.

Key Achievements

  • Performed detailed biomechanical analysis for optimal bike fit
  • Designed and 3D printed custom cable guides for improved routing
  • Implemented ceramic bearing upgrades in bottom bracket and wheels
  • Achieved 15% reduction in aerodynamic drag through component optimization

Technical Details

The project involved several key technical aspects:

  • Frame geometry analysis and sizing calculations
  • Custom component specification and compatibility verification
  • 3D modeling and printing of custom parts
  • Aerodynamic optimization through component selection and positioning

Results

The optimized build achieved significant improvements:

  • Reduced overall weight by 1.2kg while maintaining structural integrity
  • Improved power transfer efficiency through upgraded bearings
  • Enhanced comfort through optimized contact points and vibration damping
  • Better aerodynamics leading to reduced power requirements at speed

Tools & Technologies Used

  • 3D CAD software for custom part design
  • 3D printing for prototype and final parts
  • Specialized bike fitting equipment
  • Torque measurement tools for precise assembly
